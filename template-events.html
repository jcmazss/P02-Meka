Workshops

We believe in sharing ideas and working together as a community to make everyone a better model kit builder. Find a variety of topics about the building process, techniques, and tips to make your own models shine. We work with many groups, stores, and organizations to find events and workshops near you.

Nearby Events

Working with Tamiya Paint

Having problems painting? Learn from master painter, Adrian Wong, on how to paint on a budget. You'll learn how to paint using Tamiya paint with beginner techniques and learn important processes. All tools, brushes, and paints will be provided.

Skill Level: Beginner

How to Create the PERFECT Diorama

Creating the perfect environment to place your model kits are extremely important for competitions. We will teach you to find the right materials and how to put them together to create beautiful dioramas that match your models. Feel free to bring your own models (we will provide them if needed).

Skill Level: Intermediate/Advanced

Sanding 101: Using the right grit

Many model kit builders don't choose the right sanding grit when it comes to their models. This workshop will teach you various sanding techniques and tricks using different grits.

Skill Level: All levels

Mecha Q&A + Modeling Tutorial (Josh Choo)

Renowned Japanese culture blogger Josh Choo will be hosting a livestream Q&A event to teach everyone about his journeys in Japan, life as a blogger and model kit builder, and what his future plans are.

Skill Level: Beginner

Gundam Paneling Lining

Panel lining is the process of drawing fine lines onto the detailed parts of individual model components. Learn useful tricks to highlight the best details of your models. Gundam pieces will be provided to test and practice your skills.

Skill Level: Beginner

Newtype: Anime Awards 2015-16 Winners

By Timothy Ma

Newtype, the long-running monthly anime magazine published by Kadokawa, has published the final results of its 6th annual Newtype Anime Awards. Found out which anime Newtype readers gave the gold after the jump!

By some counts, there are as many as 70 new and continuing anime series on right now, and if your response to that crazy bit of knowledge is “no one can watch that many,” you are not alone, my friend. As the season wears on, even the most hardcore anime fans start dropping shows. Which series have kept the viewers’ attention, and which will be relegated to that anime dustbin of history? Charapedia has polled it readers to find out, and this is what they found.

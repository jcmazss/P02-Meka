Newtype: Anime Awards 2015-16 Winners

By Timothy Ma | July 12, 2016

Newtype, the long-running monthly anime magazine published by Kadokawa, has published the final results of its 6th annual Newtype Anime Awards. Found out which anime Newtype readers gave the gold after the jump!

By some counts, there were as many as 70 new and continuing anime series but there were clear winners and must watch shows. As the season wears on, even the most hardcore anime fans start dropping shows. Which series have kept the viewers’ attention, and which will be relegated to that anime dustbin of history? Charapedia has polled it readers to find out, and this is what they found.

  1. Gundam Build Fighters
  2. Haikyuu!!
  3. Kiss Him, Not Me
  4. High School of the Dead
  5. Occultic Nine
  6. Boruto
  7. Magic of Stella
  8. The Great Passage
  9. Touken Ranbu
  10. Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans
  11. Izetta: The Last Witch
  12. Mobile Suit Gundam Unicorn

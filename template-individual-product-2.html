With a new variant design and updated color scheme (designed by famed mecha designer Hajime Katoki), the model kit features an advanced MS Skeleton and Joints, new matte finish, and a wide array of weapons. Customer Reviews

This is awesome, I see why everyone wants it!

By Mark Leung on December 12, 2015

For a Master Grade kit, this feels more like a Perfect Grade! It's absolutely huge and the details are spot on and beautiful.

This has a bit of everything that makes Bandai so good - the sharp colors (no need for paint), great designs on the body and weapons, and great effect parts included. The details are incredibly on point which includes some nice touches like springs in the power cables, openable panels that spread out and reveal more thrusters, and the flexible armor that can move in multiple directions for better poses. Also, because this is a Katoki designed kit, you'll find lots of decals/stickers to make the kit look even more detailed. I'd suggest using a stand because standing on its own on a table seems quite unstable due to the weight. Good luck to you all!!

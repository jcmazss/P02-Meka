About Us

Who are we

Meka is an ecommerce platform to purchase model kits, tools and additional accessories. We specialize in selling Japanese mecha for beginner builders to hardcore enthusiasts. Feel free to view our member's submitted models and projects, learn new techniques, and find local meet ups. We are a passionate community of people sharing our love of model making.

Why choose us

We provide the best deals anywhere online - we'll beat any price by 10% guaranteed. We're able to provide the lowest prices because we buy directly from BANDAI. It also doesn't hurt that we're a group of ex-designers and engineers from BANDAI. Working closely with shipping companies around the world, we are able to provide the best quality shipping services.
